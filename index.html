How to play

  • Find 1 or more friends
  • everybody has 3 lives
  • Hit eachother with your dimensional mirror ball (press attack to throw)
  • You can charge your attack for a bigger projectile

Game supports GameControllers & 1 Keyboard

Default keyboard bindings are

  • Shift to attack/ok
  • space to jump
  • A for left
  • D for right

To connect a gamepad:

  • connect it to windows
  • then press a button
  • if this is a new controller please configure it first by clicking the controllers button
  • the game saves the configuration
